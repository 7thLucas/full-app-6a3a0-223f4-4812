# Product Overview

## Name
**Festa** — clean and premium; reads well across web, mobile, and a developer's
marketing materials.

## What it is
A mobile and web platform that lets property developers showcase their projects and
available units in a professional, interactive way — and converts that interest into
qualified leads and booked site visits. It is the bridge between a buyer falling for a
unit and the sales team closing the deal, replacing scattered brochures, spreadsheets,
and cold WhatsApp threads with one centralized sales engine.

## Core problem
Developers lose deals in the gap between buyer interest and sales follow-up. Property
information lives in PDFs, printed brochures, and disconnected chat threads; hot leads
go cold over a weekend; and developers have no visibility into which projects, units, or
channels actually generate inquiries and bookings.

## The outcome it sells
Sell units faster and lose fewer leads. Present properties beautifully, capture buyer
intent the moment it peaks, route qualified leads to the right agent instantly, and give
developers a clear view of what's converting.

## Target users
- **Property developers** — own the projects, manage portfolio, pricing, and inventory.
- **Real estate sales teams** — work the leads and move units to booking.
- **Property agents** — field inquiries and handle buyer conversations.
- **Home buyers and investors** — discover, compare, and shortlist units, then inquire
  or schedule a viewing.

## Core capabilities
- **Project portfolio & property listings** — a polished catalog of developments.
- **Unit catalog with detailed specifications** — floor plans, land/building size,
  bedrooms, bathrooms, facilities, and more per unit.
- **Rich media** — high-quality photos, videos, and virtual tours.
- **Interactive site plan & unit availability** — live status (available / reserved /
  sold) mapped to the site layout.
- **Pricing & payment schemes** — price lists and payment/installment plan information.
- **Lead capture & inquiry forms** — capture buyer intent at the point of interest.
- **Site visit & viewing scheduling** — let buyers book a viewing without back-and-forth.
- **Mortgage / financing calculator** — buyers estimate affordability in-app.
- **Agent contact & WhatsApp integration** — connect buyers to the right agent directly.
- **Promotions & featured projects** — surface campaigns and priority inventory.
- **Location maps & nearby amenities** — context around each project.
- **Customer accounts & saved favorites** — buyers shortlist units they love.
- **Admin dashboard** — manage projects, units, pricing, and leads.
- **Analytics dashboard** — track views, inquiries, and conversions.

## Value proposition
A centralized digital platform that helps developers present properties professionally,
generate qualified leads, and streamline the sales process from discovery to booking.

## North star
The operation this product lives or dies by is the **qualified lead captured** — a buyer
who submits an inquiry, schedules a site visit, or books a unit through the app. Views
and saved favorites feed the funnel; captured leads and booked visits are the verified
operations that move revenue.

## Tone & positioning
Premium, professional, and trustworthy — real estate is a high-consideration purchase.
Polished and visual for buyers; efficient and data-clear for developers and sales teams.

## Status
Core truth defined from the initial brief. Day-one priority confirmed: the **interactive
listings → instant lead capture** loop (browse a unit, inquire or book a viewing on the
spot, routed to an agent before interest cools) — everything else orbits this. Product
name confirmed: **Festa**. Initial app build not yet triggered.
