## Festa — Premium Property Sales Platform

**What it is:** A premium mobile + web platform that lets property developers showcase projects and available units in an interactive, professional way — and converts that interest into qualified leads and booked site visits. It replaces scattered brochures, spreadsheets, and cold WhatsApp threads with one centralized sales engine that bridges a buyer falling for a unit and the sales team closing the deal.

**Core problem:** Developers lose deals in the gap between buyer interest and sales follow-up. Property info lives in PDFs, printed brochures, and disconnected chat threads; hot leads go cold over a weekend; and developers have no visibility into which projects, units, or channels actually generate inquiries and bookings.

### Target users
- **Home buyers & investors** — discover, compare, and shortlist units, then inquire or schedule a viewing (primary day-one audience).
- **Property developers** — own projects, manage portfolio, pricing, and inventory.
- **Real estate sales teams** — work the leads and move units to booking.
- **Property agents** — field inquiries and handle buyer conversations.

### DAY-ONE CORE — build this first and make it shine
**The interactive listings → instant lead capture loop.** A buyer browses projects and units (with detailed specs: floor plans, land/building size, bedrooms, bathrooms, facilities, photos/video), falls for a unit, and inquires or books a site visit right there — routed to an agent (WhatsApp contact) before interest cools. This loop is the heart of the product; everything else orbits it. The full path must feel effortless and premium:
1. Browse a polished catalog of projects.
2. Open a project, see its units with rich media and full specs.
3. Open a unit detail page that makes the buyer fall for it (gallery, floor plan, specs, facilities, price).
4. Inquire / book a site visit / WhatsApp the agent — captured at peak intent.

### Supporting capabilities (orbit the core)
- **Interactive site plan with unit availability** — live status mapped to layout: available / reserved / sold (use clear status colors).
- **Price lists & payment schemes** — per-unit pricing and installment/payment plan info.
- **Mortgage / financing calculator** — buyers estimate affordability in-app.
- **Customer accounts & saved favorites** — buyers shortlist units they love.
- **Promotions & featured projects** — surface campaigns and priority inventory.
- **Location maps & nearby amenities** — context around each project.
- **Agent contact & WhatsApp integration** — connect buyers to the right agent directly.
- **Admin dashboard** — manage projects, units, pricing, and leads.
- **Analytics dashboard** — track views, inquiries, and conversions.

### North star metric
**Qualified leads captured** — a buyer who submits an inquiry, schedules a site visit, or books a unit through the app. Views and saved favorites feed the funnel; captured leads and booked visits are the verified operations that move revenue.

### Tone & positioning
Premium, professional, and trustworthy — real estate is a high-consideration purchase. Polished and visual for buyers; efficient and data-clear for developers and sales teams. Calm confidence, not flashy.
